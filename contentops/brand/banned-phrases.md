# Banned And Discouraged Phrases

## Hard Bans

- "AI will change everything"
- "game changer" (without specifics)
- "revolutionary" (without mechanism)
- "future of work" (generic use)
- "just add AI"
- "set and forget"
- "fully autonomous" (for enterprise claims)

## Replace With

- "operational reliability"
- "workflow ownership"
- "production guardrails"
- "context quality"
- "handoff design"
- "human-in-the-loop checkpoints"
- "evaluation discipline"
