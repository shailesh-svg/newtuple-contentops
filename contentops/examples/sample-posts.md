# Sample Newtuple-Voice Posts

## Sample 1

Most AI agent demos work. Most AI agent systems don't.

The gap is rarely model quality. It is workflow design.

When ownership is unclear, handoffs fail. When handoffs fail, teams lose trust. When trust drops, adoption stops.

If you are moving beyond pilots, map the workflow first. Then automate.

## Sample 2

The question is no longer can we build this.

The question is who owns it when it breaks on a Tuesday morning.

Enterprise AI fails less from missing capability and more from missing operational ownership.

Before your next rollout, define incident paths, escalation owners, and review checkpoints.
