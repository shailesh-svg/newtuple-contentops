# Hooks Library

## Tension Hooks

- Most AI agent demos work. Most AI agent systems don't.
- Faster output is easy. Reliable output is hard.
- Shipping a prototype is quick. Operating it is the real work.

## Misconception Hooks

- The biggest AI problem is not model quality. It is workflow ownership.
- Teams think they need better prompts. They usually need better orchestration.
- AI adoption does not fail in ideation. It fails in operations.

## Shift Hooks

- AI agents are no longer just experiments.
- The question is no longer can we build this.
- Enterprise AI has moved from capability race to reliability race.
