# Content Tracker Fields

Required columns:

- `idea_id`
- `created_at`
- `source_type`
- `source_link`
- `raw_input`
- `title`
- `draft_text`
- `bucket`
- `status` (`new`, `needs_review`, `Approved`, `Needs Revision`, `Rejected`, `scheduled`, `published`)
- `reviewer`
- `review_notes`
- `scheduled_at`
- `published_url`
- `platform`
- `hook_type`
- `voice_score`

Optional analytics columns:

- `engagement_likes`
- `engagement_comments`
- `engagement_shares`
- `reach`
- `ctr`
- `next_repurpose_action`
